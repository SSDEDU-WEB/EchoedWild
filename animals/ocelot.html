Ocelot

Least Concern

Estimated remaining: ~40,000

Habitat

Forests, scrublands, and mangroves of South and Central America, with a small population in southern Texas, preferring dense vegetation for cover.

Threats

  • Habitat loss and fragmentation
  • Illegal pet trade
  • Poaching for fur (historically)
  • Road mortality
  • Human-wildlife conflict

Conservation efforts

Protected in national parks and reserves. CITES Appendix I listing for trade protection. Habitat corridor initiatives. Research on ecology and genetics. Community-based conservation programs.

Flat-headed Cat

Endangered

Estimated remaining: ~2,500

Habitat

Lowland tropical forests, freshwater swamps, and riverbanks of the Malay Peninsula, Borneo, and Sumatra, with specialized adaptations for aquatic hunting.

Threats

  • Deforestation for palm oil plantations
  • Wetland drainage for agriculture
  • Water pollution from plantations
  • Habitat fragmentation
  • Prey depletion

Conservation efforts

Protected in national parks with wetland habitats. Research on ecology and distribution. Habitat protection initiatives. Community education programs. International conservation focus on wetland species.

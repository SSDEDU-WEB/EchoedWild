Marbled Cat

Near Threatened

Estimated remaining: ~10,000

Habitat

Tropical forests of Southeast Asia, from Nepal to Borneo, with excellent climbing abilities and preference for dense forest canopy.

Threats

  • Deforestation for logging and agriculture
  • Poaching for skins
  • Habitat fragmentation
  • Illegal wildlife trade
  • Prey depletion

Conservation efforts

Protected in national parks and reserves. Research on arboreal ecology. CITES Appendix I listing. Habitat corridor initiatives. Camera trap monitoring in forest canopies.

Pallas's Cat

Least Concern

Estimated remaining: ~58,000

Habitat

Steppes, grasslands, and rocky areas of Central Asia, from Iran to Mongolia, adapted to cold, arid environments with sparse vegetation.

Threats

  • Habitat degradation from livestock grazing
  • Poaching for fur and traditional medicine
  • Prey depletion from rodent control
  • Road mortality in developed areas
  • Disease transmission from domestic animals

Conservation efforts

Protected in national parks and reserves. Captive breeding programs in zoos. Research on ecology and behavior. Community education programs. Monitoring of population trends.

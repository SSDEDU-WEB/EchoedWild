Giant Sable Antelope

Critically Endangered

Estimated remaining: ~100

Habitat

Miombo woodlands and open savannas of central Angola, particularly in Luando Reserve and Cangandala National Park, where they browse on leaves and grasses.

Threats

  • Poaching during Angola's civil war
  • Habitat loss from agriculture
  • Hybridization with roan antelope
  • Civil unrest limiting conservation efforts
  • Small population size

Conservation efforts

Establishment of protected areas in Angola. Captive breeding and reintroduction programs. Genetic studies to prevent hybridization. Anti-poaching patrols in remaining habitats. International support for conservation initiatives.

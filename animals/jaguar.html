Jaguar

Near Threatened

Estimated remaining: ~64,000

Habitat

Rainforests, swamps, grasslands, and dry forests of Central and South America, from Mexico to Argentina, with strongest populations in Amazon basin.

Threats

  • Deforestation and habitat loss
  • Human-jaguar conflict
  • Poaching for skins and body parts
  • Prey depletion
  • Infrastructure development fragmenting habitat

Conservation efforts

Protected in national parks and reserves. Jaguar corridors initiative connecting habitats. Community-based conservation programs. Research using camera traps. International jaguar conservation alliance across 18 countries.

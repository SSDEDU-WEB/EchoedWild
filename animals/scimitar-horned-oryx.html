Scimitar-Horned Oryx

Endangered

Estimated remaining: Reintroduced populations

Habitat

Desert and semi-desert regions of North Africa including Chad, Niger, and Tunisia, preferring open grasslands, steppes, and desert fringes with access to water.

Threats

  • Overhunting for meat and horns
  • Habitat loss to livestock
  • Drought and desertification
  • Civil unrest in native range
  • Historical extinction in the wild

Conservation efforts

Successful reintroduction to Chad's Ouadi Rimé-Ouadi Achim Reserve. Large-scale breeding in protected areas. Satellite tracking of reintroduced herds. International breeding programs maintained genetic diversity. Community-based conservation initiatives.

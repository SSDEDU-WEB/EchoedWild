Bactrian Camel

Critically Endangered

Estimated remaining: ~1,000

Habitat

Desert and semi-desert regions of Mongolia and China including the Gobi Desert, surviving in extreme temperatures from -40°C to 40°C with scarce water sources.

Threats

  • Hybridization with domestic camels
  • Competition with livestock for resources
  • Mining and industrial development
  • Poaching for meat and hides
  • Climate change affecting desert ecosystems

Conservation efforts

Protected in Great Gobi Reserve A in Mongolia. Breeding programs in captivity. Genetic studies to prevent hybridization. Community-based conservation with herders. International cooperation between Mongolia and China.

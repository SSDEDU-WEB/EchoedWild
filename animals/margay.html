Margay

Near Threatened

Estimated remaining: Unknown

Habitat

Tropical and subtropical forests of Central and South America, from Mexico to Uruguay, with exceptional climbing abilities and preference for dense canopy cover.

Threats

  • Deforestation for agriculture
  • Illegal pet trade
  • Poaching for skins
  • Habitat fragmentation
  • Road mortality

Conservation efforts

Protected in national parks and reserves. Research on arboreal ecology. CITES Appendix I listing. Habitat corridor initiatives. Community education about conservation.

Sand Cat

Least Concern

Estimated remaining: Unknown

Habitat

Sandy and stony deserts of North Africa, the Middle East, and Central Asia, adapted to extreme temperatures and scarce water availability.

Threats

  • Habitat degradation from development
  • Illegal pet trade
  • Road mortality in desert areas
  • Prey depletion from rodent control
  • Climate change affecting desert ecosystems

Conservation efforts

Protected in desert reserves and national parks. Research on desert adaptations. Captive breeding programs. Community education in range countries. Monitoring of population distribution.

Golden Jackal

Least Concern

Estimated remaining: Unknown

Habitat

Varied habitats across Europe, Asia, and Africa including grasslands, woodlands, scrublands, and agricultural areas, adaptable to human presence.

Threats

  • Persecution as livestock predators
  • Road mortality
  • Habitat loss in some regions
  • Poisoning from predator control
  • Disease transmission

Conservation efforts

Protected in some national parks. Research on expanding range in Europe. Community education about ecological role. Monitoring of population trends. Habitat protection where needed.

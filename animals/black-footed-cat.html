Black-footed Cat

Vulnerable

Estimated remaining: ~13,000

Habitat

Open savannas, grasslands, and semi-deserts of Southern Africa, particularly in South Africa, Namibia, and Botswana with sparse vegetation.

Threats

  • Habitat loss to agriculture
  • Poisoning from rodent control
  • Predation by larger carnivores
  • Road mortality
  • Disease transmission from domestic cats

Conservation efforts

Protected in national parks and reserves. Captive breeding program in zoos. Research on ecology and reproduction. Community education programs. Monitoring of wild populations.

Cougar

Least Concern

Estimated remaining: ~50,000

Habitat

Varied habitats across the Americas from Canada to Chile including forests, mountains, deserts, and wetlands, with the largest range of any wild terrestrial mammal in Western Hemisphere.

Threats

  • Habitat fragmentation
  • Human-cougar conflict
  • Road mortality
  • Trophy hunting in some areas
  • Prey depletion

Conservation efforts

Protected in national parks and reserves. Research on ecology and movements. Wildlife corridors maintaining connectivity. Community education about coexistence. Hunting regulations and quotas.

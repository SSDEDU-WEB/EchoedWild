Caracal

Least Concern

Estimated remaining: Unknown

Habitat

Varied habitats across Africa, Middle East, and Central Asia including savannas, woodlands, scrublands, and semi-deserts, adaptable to different environments.

Threats

  • Habitat loss to agriculture
  • Persecution as livestock predators
  • Road mortality in developed areas
  • Illegal pet trade in some regions
  • Prey depletion in certain areas

Conservation efforts

Protected in national parks and reserves across range. Research on ecology and behavior. Community-based conflict mitigation. Monitoring populations in key areas. Education about ecological role.

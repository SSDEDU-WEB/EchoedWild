Addra Gazelle

Critically Endangered

Estimated remaining: ~300

Habitat

Sahel region of Africa including Chad, Niger, and Mali, inhabiting semi-desert grasslands, acacia savannas, and desert fringes with sparse vegetation.

Threats

  • Overhunting for meat and trophies
  • Habitat loss from desertification
  • Competition with domestic livestock
  • Political instability in range countries
  • Drought and climate change

Conservation efforts

Protected areas in Chad and Niger. Captive breeding programs in zoos worldwide. Reintroduction efforts in fenced reserves. Community-based conservation initiatives. International species survival plan.

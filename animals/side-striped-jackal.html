Side-striped Jackal

Least Concern

Estimated remaining: Unknown

Habitat

Woodlands, savannas, and forest edges of Central and Southern Africa, more omnivorous than other jackals with varied diet including fruit.

Threats

  • Persecution as poultry predators
  • Habitat loss to agriculture
  • Road mortality
  • Poisoning from predator control
  • Disease transmission

Conservation efforts

Protected in national parks and reserves. Research on ecology and diet. Community education programs. Monitoring of population distribution. Habitat protection initiatives.

Clouded Leopard

Vulnerable

Estimated remaining: ~10,000

Habitat

Tropical and subtropical forests of Southeast Asia including foothills and lower mountain slopes, preferring dense forest cover with trees for climbing.

Threats

  • Deforestation for palm oil and logging
  • Poaching for skins and body parts
  • Illegal pet trade
  • Prey depletion
  • Habitat fragmentation

Conservation efforts

Protected in national parks across range. Captive breeding programs. Research on ecology and behavior. Anti-poaching patrols in protected areas. Community education programs. Camera trap monitoring.

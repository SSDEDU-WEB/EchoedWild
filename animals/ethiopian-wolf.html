Ethiopian Wolf

Endangered

Estimated remaining: ~500

Habitat

Afroalpine grasslands and heathlands of the Ethiopian Highlands above 3,000 meters, one of the world's rarest canids and Africa's most endangered carnivore.

Threats
  • Habitat loss to agriculture
  • Disease outbreaks from domestic dogs
  • Hybridization with domestic dogs
  • Human persecution
  • Climate change affecting alpine ecosystems

Conservation efforts

Protected in national parks like Bale Mountains. Vaccination programs for domestic dogs. Community-based conservation. Research on ecology and disease. International conservation partnerships.

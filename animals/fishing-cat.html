Fishing Cat

Vulnerable

Estimated remaining: ~10,000

Habitat

Wetlands, mangroves, and riverine forests of South and Southeast Asia, specialized for aquatic hunting with webbed feet and fishing behavior.

Threats

  • Wetland destruction for development
  • Aquaculture conflicts
  • Pollution of water sources
  • Poaching for traditional medicine
  • Climate change affecting coastal habitats

Conservation efforts

Protected in wetland reserves and national parks. Research on aquatic ecology. Community-based conservation programs. Habitat restoration initiatives. International conservation partnerships.
